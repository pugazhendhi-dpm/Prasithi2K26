Who We Are

About Prasidhi '26

Prasidhi 26 is a National Level Symposium organized by the National Service Scheme (NSS) of Kongu Engineering College, Perundurai. The event serves as a platform for students, innovators, researchers, and aspiring entrepreneurs from various institutions to showcase their talents, exchange ideas, and contribute towards building a sustainable future.

With the theme "Healing the World" and the vision of "Reduce, Reuse, Recycle", Prasidhi 26 encourages participants to develop innovative solutions that address real-world environmental and social challenges. The symposium aims to inspire creativity, technical excellence, leadership, and social responsibility among the youth.

The event features a diverse range of competitions and activities, including Project Showcase, Idea to Action Plan, Bot in Action, CODE Master, The Think Tank, Entrepreneurship Challenges, Code Clash, and Creative Events. These programs provide opportunities for participants to demonstrate their technical knowledge, problem-solving abilities, teamwork, and innovative thinking.

Prasidhi 26 is more than just a symposium—it is a movement that brings together passionate minds committed to creating positive change. By fostering collaboration, innovation, and sustainability, the event seeks to empower the next generation of leaders to transform ideas into impactful actions.

Join us at Prasidhi 26 and be a part of a journey where innovation meets responsibility, and ideas become solutions for a better tomorrow.
